Our story

Dallas' Trusted Mustang Specialists Since 1998

To provide expert, honest, and affordable Mustang repair and maintenance to the Dallas community.

Mustang Service Center was founded in 1998 by lifelong Mustang enthusiast John M. with a simple mission: offer specialized, high-quality service for Mustangs at a fair price. What started as a two-bay shop has grown into a full-service facility serving thousands of Mustang owners across Dallas.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of certified technicians who share a passion for the Mustang brand. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to ensure every Mustang that leaves our shop is safe, reliable, and performing at its best. We treat every car as if it were our own.
