Overview

Your Mustang's braking and suspension systems are critical for safety and performance. We offer everything from pad replacements to full coilover installations, using top-tier components for street and track use.

What's included

  • Improved stopping power and pedal feel
  • Enhanced cornering and reduced body roll
  • Custom alignment tailored to your driving style
  • High-performance and OEM options available

How it works

1

Inspection

We check pad thickness, rotor condition, brake fluid, and suspension components for wear or damage.

2

Replacement/Upgrade

We install your chosen parts with precision, torquing all fasteners to spec and using proper procedures.

3

Test & Align

After installation, we test the brakes for proper bedding and perform a four-wheel alignment to ensure optimal handling.

Common questions

How often should I replace my Mustang's brakes?

It depends on driving habits. Typically, front pads last 30,000-50,000 miles and rear pads 40,000-60,000 miles. We inspect them at every service and advise accordingly.

Do you offer performance suspension upgrades?

Yes, we carry brands like Ford Performance, Eibach, Bilstein, and KW. We can install lowering springs, adjustable coilovers, sway bars, and more to transform your Mustang's handling.
