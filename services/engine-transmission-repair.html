Overview

From minor repairs to complete rebuilds, our technicians have the expertise to handle any engine or transmission issue. We service all generations, including the classic 289/302, modern Coyote, and the latest EcoBoost. Using OEM and high-quality aftermarket parts, we ensure your powertrain runs reliably for years to come.

What's included

  • Accurate diagnostics using factory-level tools
  • Complete engine and transmission rebuilds
  • Performance upgrades available during repair
  • 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on all work

A typical rebuild takes 2-4 weeks depending on parts availability and scope. We'll give you a timeline upfront.

Yes, we service both automatic and manual transmissions, including the 6R80, 10R80, MT-82, and T-5.
