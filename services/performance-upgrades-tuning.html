Overview

Unlock your Mustang's full potential with our performance tuning services. Using advanced dyno tuning, we calibrate your engine's ECU for optimal air/fuel ratios and ignition timing. We also install cold air intakes, exhaust systems, superchargers, and turbochargers to maximize power output. Whether you're building a track monster or a spirited street car, we ensure every modification works in harmony.

What's included

  • Significant horsepower and torque gains
  • Smoother throttle response and drivability
  • Custom calibration tailored to your driving style
  • Professional installation with warranty

Common questions

Will tuning void my warranty?

Aftermarket tuning can void your factory warranty. However, we offer tuning solutions that can be reverted to stock if needed. We always advise checking with your dealer.

How much horsepower can I expect?

Gains vary by modification. A simple tune and intake can add 15-25 HP, while a supercharger kit can add 150+ HP. We'll provide realistic estimates during consultation.
