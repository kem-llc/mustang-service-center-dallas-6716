Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced diagnostics to pinpoint and fix engine issues quickly.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment can identify issues in your Mustang's engine, transmission, and electronic systems. From check engine lights to performance problems, we provide accurate repairs with transparent pricing.

What's included

  • Factory-level diagnostic scanners
  • Experienced technicians with Ford-specific training
  • Detailed explanations of issues and repair options
  • Lifetime warranty on parts and labor

How it works

1

Diagnostic Scan

We connect advanced scanners to read fault codes and live data from your Mustang's computer systems.

2

Analysis

Our technician interprets the data and performs additional tests (compression, fuel pressure, etc.) to confirm the root cause.

3

Repair & Verify

We complete the necessary repairs, clear codes, and test-drive your Mustang to ensure everything is working properly.

Common questions

What does a diagnostic service include?

We scan all engine and transmission control modules, check for pending codes, and perform a visual inspection of related components. You receive a detailed report with recommended repairs.

Can you diagnose intermittent problems?

Yes, we can perform extended diagnostics and road tests to replicate intermittent issues. Our advanced tools capture data during the problem to identify elusive faults.
