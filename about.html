Our story

Dallas's Mustang Specialists Since 2005

To provide every Mustang owner with expert, honest, and passionate service that keeps their car performing at its best.

Mustang Service Center was founded in 2005 by master technician John Miller, a lifelong Mustang enthusiast. What started as a small two-bay shop in East Dallas quickly grew into a trusted destination for Mustang owners across the metroplex. John's philosophy was simple: treat every car as if it were your own, and never cut corners. That commitment to quality earned the shop a loyal following and a reputation for solving problems that other shops couldn't.

Today, our team includes five ASE-certified technicians with over 50 years of combined experience working exclusively on Ford Mustangs. We've invested in the latest diagnostic and repair equipment, including a Mustang-specific dyno and factory-level programming tools. While we've grown, our core values remain unchanged: transparency, craftsmanship, and a genuine love for the pony car.
