Our story

Dallas' Mustang Experts Since 1995

To provide Ford Mustang owners with expert, honest, and high-quality service that keeps their cars running at their best — whether on the street or the track.

Mustang Service Center was founded in 1995 by lifelong Mustang enthusiast John Miller. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into Dallas' premier destination for Mustang care. John's philosophy was simple: treat every car like it's your own, and never cut corners. That commitment to quality and integrity quickly earned a loyal following among local Mustang owners.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians continues that tradition. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment, ongoing training, and forge strong relationships with top parts suppliers. Whether you drive a classic '65 fastback or a 2024 Dark Horse, you can trust us to deliver the expertise and passion your Mustang deserves.
